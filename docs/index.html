Methodology

Data Pipeline

nflverse PBP FTN Charting MockDraftable

484K plays across 2016–2025, merged with participation data (personnel groupings, formation) and FTN charting data (motion, play action, screen, coverage type). Extended measurables (hand size, arm length, wingspan) scraped from MockDraftable for 155 WRs drafted rounds 1–3.

EPA Prediction Model

PyTorch Gaussian NLL 192K plays

Two-headed neural network predicting both the mean and variance of EPA per play from 20 scheme/context features. Trained with Gaussian negative log-likelihood loss so the model learns uncertainty, not just point estimates.

Quality Calibration

Ridge Regression CV-Optimal α 79 WR Profiles

Draft capital + combine measurables (including hand size and arm length) mapped to Year 1 production via Ridge regression. Model selected over OLS, Lasso, ElasticNet, and PCA through 10-fold cross-validation.

Projection Engine

Monte Carlo 2,000 Seasons Uncertainty Propagation

Each simulation samples play contexts from historical Shanahan-tree slot WR targets, applies scenario-specific adjustments (personnel, motion, screen rate), and converts EPA predictions to counting stats using Lemon-calibrated rates.
